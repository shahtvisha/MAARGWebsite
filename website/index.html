A path to equal education and career opportunities

Maarg is a not-for-profit organization facilitating language-inclusive career awareness programs for Indian underprivileged students (grades 9 to 12) through peers and professionals who volunteer to provide free mentorship and resources in order to encourage full time and part time higher education.


Under Maarg, students from underprivileged communities will have support to continue engagement in education, and industry. This will help them to alleviate themselves out of the poverty cycle that is stimulated by ignorance of opportunity in sectors of career and education.
